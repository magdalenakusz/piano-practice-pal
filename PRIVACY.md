# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: November 3, 2025

## Overview

Piano Practice Pal ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We collect NO personal data.**

The App:
- Does NOT collect any personal information
- Does NOT require account creation or login
- Does NOT track your location
- Does NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
- Does NOT use cookies or analytics
- Does NOT share any data with third parties

## Local Data Storage

All practice data (scale history, confidence ratings, practice dates) is stored **locally on your device only**. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any server
- Is not accessible to us or anyone else
- Can be exported/imported by you for backup purposes
- Can be deleted at any time by you

## Audio Generation

The App uses the Web Audio API to generate piano tones in real-time:
- No audio files are downloaded or uploaded
- All sounds are synthesized locally on your device
- No audio data is recorded or transmitted

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following open-source libraries (see LICENSES.md for full details):
- React - UI framework (does not collect data)
- VexFlow - Music notation rendering (does not collect data)
- Vite - Build tool (build-time only, no runtime tracking)

None of these libraries collect or transmit personal data.

## App Store Data

When you download the App from iOS App Store or Google Play Store:
- Apple/Google may collect download statistics
- This is governed by their privacy policies, not ours
- We do not receive identifiable user information from app stores

## Children's Privacy

The App is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Check the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after updates means you accept the changes.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Export your practice data (via the Export button)
- Delete all your practice data (via the Reset button)
- Uninstall the App at any time

**Your privacy is 100% protected because we collect ZERO data.**
